# MindGrid
**MindGrid**– AI-Powered Productivity & Habit Tracking Platform

**MindGrid** is a modern AI-powered productivity platform designed to help users stay organized, build positive habits, and achieve both short-term and long-term goals. The application combines task management, habit tracking, AI assistance, immersive focus tools, and extensive personalization into a single user-friendly experience.

## Key Features

### 📊 Smart Dashboard

The dashboard provides a complete overview of your productivity journey. It displays:

* Task completion percentage
* Daily consistency streaks
* Habit growth progress
* Active goals
* Weekly productivity graph
* Growth points earned by completing tasks and maintaining consistency

This allows users to monitor their progress and stay motivated through visual analytics.

### 🎯 Smart Focus Timer

MindGrid includes a customizable focus timer that helps users stay productive while studying or working. Users can:

* Set custom focus durations
* Select immersive focus environments such as Aquarium, Focus Tree, and other calming themes
* Create a distraction-free workspace that improves concentration

### 📝 Daily Tasks

Users can create and manage daily tasks with a unique feature that transforms their task list into a customizable wallpaper. Multiple design options, themes, colors, and layouts allow users to personalize the wallpaper, ensuring their goals remain visible every time they unlock their device.

### 📅 Productivity Calendar

The built-in calendar tracks monthly activity and visualizes progress throughout the month. It also measures weekly achievement percentages, helping users understand their productivity trends and identify areas for improvement.

### 🌱 Habit Garden

MindGrid gamifies habit building through an interactive Habit Garden. Users can track activities such as meditation, exercise, reading, running, or any custom habit. Every completed habit helps the virtual garden grow, providing a rewarding visual representation of consistency and progress.

### 🎯 AI-Powered Goal Planner

For long-term goals, users can define their objectives and create structured plans. If a user is unsure how to achieve a goal, the integrated AI automatically generates a personalized step-by-step roadmap, making long-term planning simple and achievable.

### 💡 Daily Motivation

MindGrid delivers fresh motivational quotes every day to inspire users. Quotes can also be shared easily with friends or on social media, encouraging continuous motivation and positivity.

### 🎨 Highly Customizable Appearance

The application offers extensive customization options, allowing users to personalize nearly every aspect of the interface, including:

* Themes
* Wallpapers
* Fonts
* Colors
* Layouts
* Overall visual design

This creates a unique experience tailored to each user's preferences.

## Purpose

MindGrid is designed to make productivity enjoyable by combining AI, habit tracking, gamification, data visualization, and deep personalization. Rather than simply managing tasks, the platform encourages users to build lasting habits, remain consistent, and achieve their goals through intelligent assistance and engaging visual feedback.
